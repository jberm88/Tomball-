Tomball City Guide

City Details

Tomball is a city located in Harris County, Texas. It is known for its rich history and community events.

Date Established: 1907

Population: Approximately 11,000

Parks

  • Burroughs Park
  • Kleb Woods

Main Events

The Tomball German Festival, held every October, celebrates the city's German heritage.

Other events include the Tomball BBQ Festival and the Christmas Parade.
